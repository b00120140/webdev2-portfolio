      Lines In The Sand

      MIAMI, FLORIDA 11:53PM

      Near a long stretch of beach along the Florida coastline, a man stood on the roof of a tall building with a view of the city. He was talking on his phone, clearly joking with the person on the other end; "Ha-ha, exactly! And while that.... Hang on." He paused, took the phone from his ear to check the screen. Someone else was calling him. "Yeah, I got another call coming in. I'll call you back," he hung up and answered the other caller, "Hello? Of course, everything is running right on time." The conversation was quickly taking a negative turn as the caller was clearly angry about something. "Yes, I know what you told me but he was in no way involved in the movement of the goods so you, my friend, have nothing to..." He stopped mid-sentence, in shock of what he'd just been told. "Wait. Say that again. What do you mean 'half the shipment is missing'? That's impossible. How did that happen? Yes. Yes. Okay. No, don't worry.... I'll handle it." The man turned around and hung up the phone with anger in his eyes...
